About Srikanth

Strategy Over Software.
Outcomes Over Outputs.

I'm a Dallas-based AI and RPA transformation advisor with 18 years of hands-on experience at the intersection of enterprise technology and business operations. My career spans two distinct phases: 9 years in large-scale automation for the Telecom industry, followed by deep specialization in RPA platforms — UiPath, Automation Anywhere, and Blue Prism — and the last 4 years focused exclusively on AI, ML, NLP, and Large Language Models.

I've delivered transformation programs across Telecom, Healthcare, and Finance — industries where the cost of getting AI wrong is high and the pressure to move fast is relentless. As the founder of Shiro Technologies (AI engineering and SaaS development) and Startekk (technology consulting and intelligent automation), I bring both the strategic clarity to advise at board level and the technical depth to hold vendors accountable.

I work directly with COOs, CTOs, and VP-level operations leaders — not through layers of junior analysts. Every engagement is led by me, grounded in real-world implementation experience, and designed to produce decisions your organization can act on immediately.
