The Call

What Happens on Your
Strategy Call

01

You Talk, I Listen

The first 10 minutes belong to you. I want to understand your business, your current state, and the specific pressure you're under — before I say a word about solutions.

02

Honest Diagnosis

Based on what I hear, I'll share my honest read of where you are — gaps, risks, and the highest-leverage opportunities given your situation and industry context.

03

Two or Three Clear Actions

You'll leave the call with specific, actionable next steps — not a proposal for a six-month engagement you didn't ask for. If a deeper engagement makes sense, we'll discuss it directly.

04

Fit or No Fit

If my services aren't the right fit for where you are, I'll tell you — and point you toward what is. I'd rather build a long-term relationship than force a short-term engagement.

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I hear back after submitting this form?
Within 1 business day. All inquiries go directly to [email protected] and are reviewed personally — not by a receptionist or assistant.
We're a small business. Is this just for large enterprises?
Not at all. A significant part of my work is with small and mid-market businesses — companies with 50 to 2,000 employees that need strategic AI and RPA guidance but can't justify a Big 4 engagement. I've designed my services specifically for this segment.
We already have an RPA program. Can you still help?
Yes — often this is where I add the most value. If you have an existing RPA program, I can assess the health of your automation portfolio, identify technical debt and underperforming bots, and build a modernization roadmap that prepares you for AI-augmented workflows.
Are you vendor-neutral, or do you have platform affiliations?
I am completely vendor-neutral. I have hands-on experience with UiPath, Automation Anywhere, and Blue Prism, which means I can evaluate any of them objectively based on what fits your environment — not based on referral fees or certifications.
What does a Fractional AI Advisor engagement look like?
It varies based on your needs, but typically includes monthly strategy sessions with your executive team, advisory support on AI vendor decisions and contract negotiations, attendance at key steering committee meetings, and quarterly briefings on the AI landscape relevant to your industry. Think of it as a senior AI strategist on retainer — without the full-time cost.
We're not ready to buy anything — can we still reach out?
Absolutely. The strategy call is not a sales call. If you're in early-stage evaluation, just trying to understand what AI should mean for your business, or want a second opinion on something you've been told by a vendor — that's exactly what this call is for.
Do you work with clients outside of Dallas / Texas?
Yes. While I'm based in Dallas, TX, I work with clients nationally and can conduct all assessments and advisory engagements remotely. On-site workshops and executive sessions can be arranged for the right engagement.
